Uses of Computer Science in Automated Vehicles

There are many ways that Computer Science has aided the field of Autonomous Vehicles. These include:

Automated navigation using GPS and Wi-Fi

Advancements in computer science, physics and rocket science have allowed for a global GPS network to be established, this allows for easier navigation and better location services. This also allows autonomous vehicles to calculate routes to follow automatically.

Automated obstacle detection and traversal

Machine learning has allowed for vehicles to learn how to detect obstacles, which can then influence the actions of a vehicle to adjust its path and decision making. If applied correctly hardware and software can be used to reduce traffic accidents through less error prone decision making in comparison to humans.

Vehicle to vehicle communication

Due to advances in digital wireless communication automated vehicles could potentially communicate between each other in the future to notify each other of any upcoming obstacles or sudden braking, allowing for even safer driving conditions in comparison to humans in addition to faster and less error prone decision making.
