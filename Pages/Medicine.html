Uses of Computer Science in Medicine

There are many ways that Computer Science is used in Medicine. These include:

Magnetic Resonance Imaging

This is an imaging technique that is used to produce 3D images that can be examined by a dcotor in order to make a diagnosis on a patient. Computers are used to piece together the thousands of scan images and create a 3D image of electrical activity in that region.

Computer Networking

This allows patient records to be shared much more quickly than previously possible
