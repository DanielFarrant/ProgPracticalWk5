Uses of Computer Science in Medicine

There are many ways that Computer Science is used in Medicine. These include:

Magnetic Resonance Imaging

This is an imaging technique that is used to produce 3D images that can be examined by a doctor in order to make a diagnosis on a patient. Computers are used to piece together the thousands of scan images and create a 3D image of electrical activity in that region.

Computer Networking

This allows patient records to be shared much more quickly than previously possible, which allows patients to be treated more quickly, especially when they are not near their local hospital

Spectroscopy

The spectrum from the spectroscopy test is tested against a database of known substances, helping doctors to determine a substance more quickly, so they can treat the patient as soon as possible

Research

Computers are used to run medical simulations in order to find cures to diseases such as AIDS and Alzheimer's
