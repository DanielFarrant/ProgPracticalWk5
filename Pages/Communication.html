Uses of Computer Science in Communication

There are many ways that Computer Science has aided the field of Communication. These include:

Reduction in size of communication devices

Moore's Law has led to a decrease in the overall size of computers, making it possible for people to carry phones in their pockets, leading to a more interconnected soceity

New methods of Communication

The creation of the internet has facilated the rise of video chat clients, where family members on other sides of the world can have a face-to-face conversation with their loved ones

Ubiquity of cameras

Advancements in video technology mean that almost everyone's phones are equipped with high definition cameras, allowing events, good and bad, to seen by the whole world
